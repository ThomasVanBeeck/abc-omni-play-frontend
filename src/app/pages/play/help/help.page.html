User Manual

Getting Started

PLAY Introduction:
PLAY, integrated within the ABC-Omni application, offers employees an easy way to publish and manage company activities. It enables users to browse available events, confirm participation, and record scores. Each user accumulates experience points, encouraging interaction and simplifying the planning and execution of employee activities.

Symbols:
  Default User Symbol:
This symbol represents individual users. It appears in all lists throughout the application, indicating that tapping it will reveal more detailed information about that specific user.

  Blue Activity Symbol:
This symbol is used to represent activities. Found in all activity lists, tapping this symbol provides access to more detailed information about the activity.

  Yellow Ownership Symbol:
This symbol indicates that you are the owner of an activity. Activities that you do not own are marked with a blue symbol.

  Experience Points (XP) Symbol:
This star symbol denotes Experience Points (XP). It is typically associated with user profiles or score displays, indicating accumulated XP.

Home

Summary:
This screen provides a quick overview of your most recent upcoming activities and your recent history.

Full Schedule:
View all your upcoming activities here, including those you're participating in and those you've published.

Full History:
This screen displays all activities you've taken part in, along with the final results you achieved.

Activities

Activities Overview:
This screen displays all publicly available upcoming activities. Tap any activity in the list to view its detailed overview. To add a new activity, simply tap the corresponding button at the top of the screen.

Details:
You can navigate to an activity's details screen by tapping an activity which is shown in the Activities overview or by looking at the different Home screens, such as Summary, Full Schedule and Full History screens.

Participating:
If you want to participate, navigate to the details screen of the activity and tap the corresponding button to join an activity.

Leaving:
Once you have joined an activity, you can cancel your participation by tapping the corresponding button to leave the activity. This button can be found at the details screen and replaces the button that is used to join an activity.

Create/Edit Activity:
Fill in all fields with the correct details. When you're done, tap the button at the top to publish or save your changes.

Cancel Activity:
To cancel an activity, first navigate to its detailed overview screen. Tap the "Edit Activity" button at the top. On the next screen, you'll find a "Cancel Activity" button (which replaces the "Cancel" button shown during creation). Tap this button to cancel your activity. WARNING: Once an activity is cancelled, it cannot be recovered.

Remove Participants:
From your activity's details screen, you can remove users. Locate the participant list at the bottom, then tap the red icon beside the user you want to remove.

Enter Scores:
Activity owners are responsible for entering scores once an activity ends. To do so, navigate to your activity's details screen and tap the designated button. For team activities, begin by filling in the team results, then accurately assign each participant to their respective team. For individual activities, establish the ranking for every participant. Double-check all entries for accuracy before submission.

Users

Users Overview:
This screen displays a list of all registered users. Tap any user in the list to navigate to their specific profile, where you can view their Summary, Full Schedule, and Full History.

Scores

Scores Overview:
This screen displays a list of all registered users. Tap any user in the list to navigate to their specific profile, where you can view their Summary, Full Schedule, and Full History.

Rankings

Ranked by XP:
This screen presents a global ranking of users based on their accumulated experience points (XP). Users are ordered from the highest to the lowest XP, reflecting their overall performance across all activities they've participated in.
